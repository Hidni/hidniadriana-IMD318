I studied at SK Methodist Klang from the age of 7 to 12, where I built a strong foundation for my education. During my time there, I learned important skills. My years at SK Methodist Klang were an important part of my early education and personal development.

The program has given me a solid foundation in managing information systems, data organization, and technology, preparing me for a successful career in the field of information management.
